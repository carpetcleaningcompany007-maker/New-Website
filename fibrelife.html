Three package structure

Built for quoting and repeat maintenance

  • FibreLife Care for regular appearance improvement and fast drying maintenance.
  • FibreLife Protect for cleaning plus added protection and easier ongoing upkeep.
  • FibreLife Restore for more heavily soiled carpets needing a stronger reset.
Why it helps

A better way to sell commercial carpet care

Commercial customers respond well to systems, not just one off service descriptions. FibreLife gives you a cleaner way to explain your process, your maintenance options and the difference between appearance cleaning, protection and deeper restorative work. That makes the service feel more premium and more deliberate.

Where FibreLife fits

It works especially well for offices, receptions, meeting rooms, hospitality spaces, communal areas and sites that want carpets looking presentable with minimal disruption. The page can also support quoting for regular maintenance programmes instead of only one off cleans.

What to say on site visits

Use FibreLife as the umbrella name, then explain which level suits the traffic, soil load and expectations of the site. That keeps the conversation simple and makes pricing easier to tier.
