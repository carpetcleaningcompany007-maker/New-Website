The Carpet Cleaning Company provides professional carpet cleaning, upholstery cleaning, rug cleaning, certified moth treatment, specialist stain removal and commercial carpet cleaning across Ludlow, Shrewsbury, Hereford, Worcester and surrounding areas. We use a proper professional process with inspection, dry soil removal, targeted pre treatment, counter rotating brush agitation and hot water extraction where appropriate. The goal is to remove embedded dirt, improve the feel of the fibres, reduce residues and leave carpets and furnishings looking noticeably better rather than simply damp and temporarily brighter.

This website is structured around the way local customers actually search. Some people search for carpet cleaning in Shrewsbury. Others search for sofa cleaning in Ludlow, rug cleaning near Hereford, commercial carpet cleaning in Worcester or carpet moth treatment in a surrounding town. The site therefore combines service pages with town pages. That creates much stronger relevance than a single homepage trying to rank for everything at once.

We work in homes, rental properties, holiday lets, offices, pubs, hotels, care settings and other commercial premises. Whether the job is a lounge carpet, a full house, a sofa suite, a wool rug, specialist stain work or a commercial maintenance clean, the focus is always on honest advice, proper process and clear quoting.
