FAQ

Questions about Kidderminster stain removal

Can every stain be removed?

No. Some stains are permanent or have changed the fibre or dye. The aim is always the best safe improvement possible, with honest advice.

Do you deal with pet accidents and odours?

Yes, stain removal enquiries often include odour concerns and these can be addressed as part of the treatment plan.

Should I scrub the stain before calling?

Usually not. Aggressive scrubbing can spread or set the stain. It is usually better to blot and get advice.

Is this service only for Kidderminster town centre?

No. The page targets Kidderminster but also supports surrounding areas and villages.
