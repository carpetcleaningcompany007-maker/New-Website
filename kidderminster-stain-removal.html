FAQ

Frequently asked questions about Kidderminster Stain Removal for Carpets, Rugs and Upholstery

Should I blot the stain first?

Yes. Blotting gently with a clean towel is usually safer than scrubbing it deeper into the fibre.

Can older stains still improve?

Yes, older stains can sometimes still improve, although results depend on what caused the stain and how long it has been there.

Do odours come out as well?

They often can, especially when the cleaning plan addresses both the visible stain and the smell.
