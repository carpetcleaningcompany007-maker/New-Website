Kidderminster Upholstery Cleaning

Kidderminster upholstery cleaning for sofas, chairs and soft furnishings that need a proper refresh.

Upholstery often collects body oils, dust, food residue, pet hair and everyday grime gradually enough that people stop noticing how much it has changed. A good upholstery clean can transform the whole feel of a room without replacing the furniture.

Whether you have a family sofa that has seen years of use, dining chairs that need lifting, or upholstered seating in a waiting room or office, the key is using the right cleaning approach for the fabric. That means looking at condition, use level and likely causes of the staining before choosing the process.
