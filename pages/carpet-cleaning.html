Our carpet cleaning service is designed for people who want more than a quick rinse over. A professional carpet carries traffic lane soil, fine grit, oils, food residue, airborne dust and general wear patterns deep in the pile. If that contamination is left there, the fibres begin to look flat, grey and tired even when the room is otherwise clean. We deal with that build up using a structured process that includes dry soil removal, fibre safe pre treatment, counter rotating brush agitation and hot water extraction with a neutralising rinse. This matters because most carpets do not just look dirty on the surface. Soil sits between and around the fibres, especially in walkways, entrances, under dining areas and in front of seating. Agitation helps separate the fibres and release the trapped dirt before extraction. That is why our system is suited both to everyday domestic carpet cleaning and to more challenging work in rental properties, holiday lets and commercial settings. The aim is always the same: better appearance, better feel underfoot and a cleaner overall finish without sticky residue left behind. We clean synthetic carpets, many wool carpets and mixed fibre floor coverings across Ludlow, Shrewsbury, Hereford, Worcester and surrounding areas. Whether you need one room cleaned, a full property refreshed or a heavier restorative clean after years of build up, we tailor the process to the fibre and the condition. We can also advise on stain treatment, odour issues, maintenance scheduling and when replacement is or is not truly necessary. In many cases, a professional clean saves the cost of new flooring and dramatically improves the look of the room.

Frequently asked questions

How often should carpets be professionally cleaned?
For most homes, every 6 to 12 months is a sensible benchmark, with more frequent cleaning for high traffic homes, pets, rental properties and busy commercial areas.

Do you clean wool carpets?
Yes, where suitable. Wool needs the right chemistry, controlled moisture and realistic fibre specific care.

Will carpet cleaning remove all stains?
Not every stain can be removed fully, especially where dye damage or long term fibre change is present, but many stains improve significantly with professional treatment.
