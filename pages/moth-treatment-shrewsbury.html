Carpet moth problems are often noticed only after damage has already started. Small bald patches, loose fibres along edges, activity under furniture and thinning areas on wool carpets or rugs can all point to textile pest activity. Our certified moth treatment service in Shrewsbury is designed to deal with this issue properly and to give customers a specialist local option rather than a generic spray and hope approach.

We assess where the activity is happening, the type of textile involved and whether treatment is needed on carpets, rugs, upholstery or a wider room area. Advice on vacuuming, preparation and follow up care is also important because successful moth work is not just about applying a product. It is about dealing with the conditions that let the problem continue.

Moth treatment is a particularly valuable service for older homes, wool carpets, fitted rugs and rooms where furniture does not move often.
