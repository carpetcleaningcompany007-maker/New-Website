Rug Cleaning Shrewsbury

Specialist rug cleaning in Shrewsbury for everyday rugs and statement pieces

Professional local service with stronger SEO copy

Rugs often carry far more visible soiling than fitted carpets because they take concentrated foot traffic, food spill risk and pet contact in a smaller area. Our rug cleaning service in Shrewsbury is designed for everyday household rugs, statement rugs and more delicate pieces that need a more considered approach than a general carpet clean.

We assess the fibre, construction and condition of the rug before cleaning. That helps us decide how aggressively we can work the piece and which products are most suitable. For many rugs the aim is to improve appearance, lift dry soil, reduce odour and bring the colour back to life without leaving residues behind. Rugs in entrances, lounges and dining spaces often respond particularly well when years of embedded dirt are flushed out properly.

Customers in Shrewsbury often book rug cleaning alongside carpet cleaning and upholstery cleaning to lift the whole room together. We also help with stain related problems, pet accidents and moth concerns where wool content makes a rug more vulnerable.
