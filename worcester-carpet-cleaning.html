How long do carpets take to dry in Worcester?

Drying time depends on fibre type, airflow and how heavily soiled the carpet is, but many domestic carpets are touch dry much sooner than people expect and some can dry in around two to four hours.

Do you clean stairs and landings?

Yes. Stairs, landings, bedrooms, lounges and hallways are all common parts of Worcester jobs and can be quoted as individual areas or as part of a full house clean.

Can you help with odours as well as soil?

Yes. If the carpet has picked up pet smells, stale odours or everyday living smells, treatment can be tailored to the issue as part of the clean.
