How long do carpets take to dry?

Drying time depends on the carpet, level of soiling, airflow and the cleaning process used. Some carpets can dry quite quickly, while others take longer depending on conditions.

Yes. Upholstery cleaning is available as well as carpet cleaning, which is useful if you want the whole room brought back up together.
